About Me

Since March 2020, I have been a Large Model Expert at Intellifusion, working on large language models, multimodal foundation models, efficient inference, and real-world AI products.

From September 2018 to February 2020, I was an AI Researcher at Noah's Ark Lab, Huawei, where I worked on deep learning for time series prediction and mobile intelligence applications.

I received my Ph.D. in Information Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2018, advised by Prof. Minghua Chen. From June 2017 to December 2017, I visited the Department of Computing Mathematical Sciences at California Institute of Technology, hosted by Prof. Adam Wierman. I obtained my B.Eng. from USTC in 2014, advised by Prof. Wenyi Zhang.
